This is the **Notes** category — shorter posts, reading notes, and everyday
learnings.

## Adding a note

Create a file in `_posts/` named `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`:

```markdown
---
title: "Your title"
date: 2026-06-29
categories:
  - Notes
tags:
  - some-tag
---

Your content in Markdown.
```

Push to `main` and it shows up under the **Notes** category.
