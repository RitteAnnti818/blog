This is the **Research** category. I'll post paper notes, ideas, and experiment
logs here.

## Adding a research post

Create a file in `_posts/` named `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` with this front matter:

```markdown
---
title: "Your title"
date: 2026-06-29
categories:
  - Research
tags:
  - some-tag
---

Your content in Markdown.
```

Push to `main` and it shows up under the **Research** category.
