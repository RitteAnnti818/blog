This is the **Notes** section — shorter posts, reading notes, and everyday
learnings.

## Adding a note

Create a file in the `_notes/` folder named `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`:

```markdown
---
title: "Your title"
date: 2026-06-29
category: Notes
---

Your content in Markdown.
```

Push to `main` and it appears under the **Notes** section in the sidebar.
