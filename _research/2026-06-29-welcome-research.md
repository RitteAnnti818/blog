This is the **Research** section. I'll post paper notes, ideas, and experiment
logs here.

## Adding a research post

Create a file in the `_research/` folder named `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` with this
front matter:

```markdown
---
title: "Your title"
date: 2026-06-29
category: Research
---

Your content in Markdown.
```

Push to `main` and it appears under the **Research** section in the sidebar.
